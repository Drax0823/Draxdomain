DRAX ASSISTANT

"Drax AI Assistant" is an innovative project developed using Python, HTML, CSS, and Java, aimed at enhancing user productivity through its versatile capabilities. With over 30 live tasks seamlessly integrated, Drax offers an array of functionalities spanning various domains. From managing daily schedules and setting reminders to retrieving real-time weather updates and generating dynamic reports, Drax ensures efficiency in both personal and professional spheres. Its intuitive interface and robust backend empower users to interact effortlessly, leveraging AI-driven insights to streamline workflows and optimize time management.
