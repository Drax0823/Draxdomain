Jarvis AI Assitant

"Jarvis AI" is an ambitious Python project inspired by intelligent virtual assistants like those in popular culture. Built using natural language processing (NLP) libraries such as NLTK and spaCy, and leveraging machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch, Jarvis AI aims to provide a seamless human-computer interaction experience. It incorporates speech recognition and synthesis to enable voice commands and responses, allowing users to perform tasks such as setting reminders, retrieving information from the web, controlling smart home devices, and even engaging in casual conversation. Jarvis AI represents a significant step towards personalized AI assistants, demonstrating the power of AI in enhancing everyday productivity and convenience.
