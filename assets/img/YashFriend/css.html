Bugatti Chiron Super Sport

The Bugatti Chiron Super Sport is an ultra-high-performance hypercar that pushes the boundaries of speed and luxury. It is equipped with an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine producing a staggering 1,578 horsepower.

Renowned for its aerodynamic design and advanced engineering, the Chiron Super Sport accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in just around 2.4 seconds and has a top speed electronically limited to 440 km/h.
