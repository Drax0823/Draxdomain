Bugatti Centodieci

Bugatti Centodieci is a limited-edition hypercar built to commemorate the 110th anniversary of Bugatti. It features a distinctive design inspired by the iconic EB110 and is powered by an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine producing 1,600 horsepower.

The Centodieci accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in just 2.4 seconds and has a top speed of 380 km/h. Only 10 units of this exclusive hypercar will be produced, making it a rare and highly sought-after collector's item.
