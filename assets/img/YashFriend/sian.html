Lamborghini Sian Roadster

The Lamborghini Sian Roadster is a hybrid supercar known for its striking design and advanced technology. It is powered by a 6.5-liter V12 engine combined with a mild-hybrid system, producing a total of 819 horsepower.

This combination allows the Sian Roadster to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in just under 2.9 seconds, making it one of the fastest Lamborghinis ever produced. Its futuristic styling and cutting-edge engineering make it a true masterpiece of automotive innovation.
