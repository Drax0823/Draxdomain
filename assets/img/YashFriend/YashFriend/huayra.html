Car Information

Pagani Huayra

The Pagani Huayra is an exotic Italian hypercar known for its craftsmanship, performance, and attention to detail. It is powered by a 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine developed by Mercedes-AMG, producing around 730 horsepower.

With a striking design that combines aerodynamic efficiency with artistic flair, the Huayra can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in approximately 3 seconds. Its top speed exceeds 370 km/h, making it one of the fastest and most exclusive hypercars in the world.
