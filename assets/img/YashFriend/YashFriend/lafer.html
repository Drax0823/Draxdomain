Car Information

La Ferrari

La Ferrari is a legendary hybrid hypercar from Ferrari, combining power and elegance. It is equipped with a 6.3-liter V12 engine paired with an electric motor, delivering a combined output of 950 horsepower.

The hybrid powertrain allows La Ferrari to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in less than 3 seconds and achieve a top speed exceeding 350 km/h. Its advanced aerodynamics and innovative technologies contribute to its exceptional performance and driving dynamics.
