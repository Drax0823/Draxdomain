Car Information

Rolls-Royce Boattail

The Rolls-Royce Boattail is a bespoke and highly customizable luxury car, designed as a modern interpretation of classic coachbuilding traditions. It features a unique rear deck inspired by luxury yachts, with exquisite craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Each Boattail is tailored to the specific preferences of its owner, showcasing Rolls-Royce's commitment to luxury and exclusivity. It is powered by a robust engine delivering smooth and effortless performance, ensuring a luxurious driving experience.
