Car Information

McLaren P1

The McLaren P1 is a hybrid hypercar that blends cutting-edge technology with exhilarating performance. It features a 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine combined with an electric motor, producing a combined output of over 900 horsepower.

With advanced aerodynamics and lightweight materials, the P1 delivers exceptional handling and acceleration. It accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in under 3 seconds and has a top speed electronically limited to 350 km/h.
