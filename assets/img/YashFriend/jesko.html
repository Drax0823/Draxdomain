Car Information

Koenigsegg Jesko

The Koenigsegg Jesko is a high-performance track-focused hypercar known for its cutting-edge technology and aerodynamic design. It is powered by a twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing over 1,600 horsepower, making it one of the most powerful cars in its class.

With advanced aerodynamics and lightweight construction, the Jesko offers exceptional handling and acceleration. It accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in under 3 seconds and has a top speed exceeding 480 km/h, setting new benchmarks in performance and engineering.
