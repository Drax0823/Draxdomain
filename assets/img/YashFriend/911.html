Car Information

Porsche 911 GT3

The Porsche 911 GT3 is a high-performance sports car designed for track enthusiasts and driving purists. It is powered by a naturally aspirated 4.0-liter flat-six engine producing around 510 horsepower.

Known for its precise handling and exhilarating driving experience, the 911 GT3 can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in approximately 3.4 seconds and has a top speed of over 320 km/h.
